My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

An Academy Award-winning adaptation of Mario Puzo's novel about the Corleone family.

Oppenheimer

During World War II, Lieutenant General Leslie Groves appoints physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer to a task force developing the Manhattan Project, whose goal is to make the first atomic bomb.

Social Network

Mark Zuckerberg, a Harvard student, creates the social network that will eventually become Facebook, but receives accusations from the co-founder and two brothers who maintain that the idea was his.
